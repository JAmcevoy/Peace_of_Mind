Mindfulness Meditation

Focus: Observing your thoughts, sensations, and emotions without judgment.

How to do it: Sit or lie down in a comfortable position. Close your eyes and pay attention to your breath or bodily sensations. When your mind wanders, gently bring your focus back to your breath or sensations.

Guided Meditation

Focus: Visualizing a peaceful scene or following a recorded meditation guide.

How to do it: Listen to a guided meditation recording or use visualization techniques to imagine a relaxing scenario.

Mantra Meditation

Focus: Repeating a word, phrase, or sound (mantra) to quiet the mind.

How to do it: Choose a mantra and repeat it silently or audibly, focusing your attention on the sound and its vibrations.

Walking Meditation

Focus: Bringing mindfulness to the act of walking.

How to do it: Walk slowly and deliberately, paying attention to each step and your breath. It can be done indoors or outdoors.
